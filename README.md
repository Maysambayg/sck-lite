# SCK-lite

Minimal coherence-governed reasoning kernel.

---

## What this is

SCK-lite is a small experimental decision engine designed to:

- Fuse multiple uncertain inputs
- Detect drift from baseline
- Measure disagreement across sources
- Score overall integrity/risk
- Decide: ACT / REVIEW / HOLD

---

## Why

Most AI systems are over-reactive and over-generative.

This project explores a different direction:

> Improving decision quality by enforcing coherence and restraint.

---

## Status

- Version: v0.1 (prototype)
- Not validated
- Not production-ready
- No claims of novelty or superiority

---

## Core Components

- Fusion (weighted)
- Drift detection
- Innovation (disagreement)
- Entropy (uncertainty)
- Integrity scoring
- Decision gating
